Want to learn more about SPF?

What is SPF?

SPF lists which mail servers are allowed to send email for a domain.

SPF Qualifiers

Qualifiers tell receivers how strict to be: pass, fail, softfail, neutral.

How SPF Works

The receiver checks the sender IP against the domain’s SPF DNS record.

Why SPF Matters

It reduces spoofing by limiting who can send mail for your domain.

SPF in Digital Forensics

SPF results in headers help show if a message was likely spoofed.
